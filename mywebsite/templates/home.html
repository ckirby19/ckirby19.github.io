About Me

Conor Kirby

Engineer & Researcher

I am a multi-disciplinary Engineer with a BSc in Mechanical Engineering & Physics from MIT and currently pursuing a MSc in Advanced Mechanical Engineering at Imperial College London.

I am interested in technology for those with neurological and neurodevelopmental disorders. I aspire to create things that enable independence and empower the user.

I have tried to design this website to be as accessible as possible. Clicking on the microphone icon will also enable Voice Navigation (it must be clicked each time to use it at the moment) and by saying the name of one of the pages below, it will open. IPA is also given for pronunciation. If you have any difficulties navigating this website, please let me know. The website is currently not optimised for mobile, so please take a look on desktop if you can. I will try to change that one day...
