My Design Philosophy

Designing with accessibility in mind always leads to better design. As an engineer and creator, it is my responsibility to ensure that what I create can be used by everyone.

When it came time to redesigning my engineering portfolio, I realised that it was the perfect opportunity to explore accessible web design, with the goal of understanding what the digital world looks and feels like for other people.
